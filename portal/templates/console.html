Patients & rooms

One EHR for the whole session. One bed is a single patient; add beds for a multi-patient room — each bed gets its own join code. You'll pick each bed's patient in the next step.

Scenario

Pick a scenario for each bed — that selects the bed's patient and pre-fills its characters.

Characters

Characters are audio-only by default (voice, runs on any tablet). Turn on avatar per character for a 3D animated face.

ⓘ Avatar hardware & cost

Audio-only (default): flat portrait + voice. Any low-cost tablet with a browser + microphone — no special hardware.

Animated avatar (3D rig): a live 3D face that lip-syncs. Needs a WebGPU-capable tablet (a recent iPad / high-end Android), more GPU & memory, and a larger first-load download (~100 MB of face + voice assets, cached after the first open). Choose it only for beds where you've budgeted those tablets.

Scenario characters

The cast that comes with each bed's scenario — they stay at that bed. The patient is always included; uncheck to drop a cast member. Toggle “avatar” for a face.

Shared characters — universal, at every bed

A common doctor, allied-health team, etc. available across all beds.

Devices

Assign devices to each bed — basic and advanced. They're minted (QR codes) at launch and appear in the cockpit ready to scan on the bedside tablets.
